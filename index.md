## Currently working on

- **Predicting Black Hole X-ray Emission from Multimodal Data**

  A probabilistic [normalizing-flow head on frozen AION embeddings](https://github.com/RoccoDiTella/erosita-desi-aion-flow) that predicts a black hole's X-ray flux from optical spectra, imaging, and infrared photometry — measuring how much X-ray information is carried by non-X-ray views of its host galaxy, and what that says about the link between accretion, gas, and dust.

## Some previous projects

- **Honest LLMs through introspection** \| [Luciana Ferrer](https://www.liaa.dc.uba.ar/equipo/luciana-ferrer/), [LIAA](https://www.liaa.dc.uba.ar/)

  Automatic detection of hallucinations in generative language models using grey-box calibration and white-box probing, to produce statistically sound measures of model confidence.

- **Visual Search** \| [Kreiman Lab](https://klab.tch.harvard.edu/), Harvard Medical School

  Modelling human visual attention with convolutional neural networks — testing whether networks trained to find a target in a cluttered scene implicitly reproduce human gaze and search behaviour (the "Finding Waldo" problem).
